Finding an element of settlement and taking into account the desire of the parties to explore the possibility of settling the issues amicably, we directed the parties to appear before the Supreme Court Mediation Centre. The mediation has fructified and a settlement agreement dated 05.01.2024 has been produced before us. Taking note of the fact that the matter which is sought to be transferred is nothing but an offshoot of the matrimonial dispute between the petitioner No.1 and her husband-Mr. Abhay Sharma, we are of the view that the settlement agreement can be accepted and Signature Not Verified Digitally signed by Vijay Kumar Date: 2024.02.06 appropriate orders can be passed. 17:11:56 IST Reason: 1 3. On going through settlement agreement, we find that though the husband of petitioner No.1-Mr. Abhay Sharma is not a party to this proceeding, he is also a signatory to the settlement agreement. Needless to say that in the such circumstances, he is also bound by the settlement agreement to take appropriate steps to comply with the obligation flowing from the settlement agreement. In that view of the matter we are inclined to dispose of this transfer petition, taking into account the terms and conditions of the settlement agreement.
