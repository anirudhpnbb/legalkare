In this matter allegations are made against present applicants who were the Directors of Pachora Peoples Cooperative Bank Limited Pachora, District Jalgaon at the relevant time. Accused No.8 had worked as General Manager for some time. Allegations are made that these applicants illegally appointed one Advocate Shri.
