Arcot N.Veerasamy , 1994 SCC Online Kar 293 : 1995 Cri LJ 1922 in the case of Prabhu Chawla and Others Vs. A.U.Sheriff and in the case of AVS Achuthanand Vs. Kamalanadh. In the result, this Criminal Original Petition is allowed. The proceedings in C.C.No.656 of 2017 on the file of the learned Judicial Magistrate – I, Tirupur is quashed.
