## Features

- **User-Friendly Interface**: Navigate through legal resources with ease.
- **Legal Consultation Booking**: Schedule consultations with certified legal professionals.
- **Resource Library**: Access a comprehensive library of legal documents, articles, and guides.
- **Case Management**: Manage your legal cases efficiently with our intuitive dashboard.
- **Notifications & Reminders**: Stay updated with important dates and updates related to your cases.
- **Secure Data Handling**: Ensuring your data privacy and security with industry-standard protocols.
